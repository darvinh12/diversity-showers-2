Frequently asked questions

Discover the technical specifications, design possibilities, and maintenance requirements for our premium architectural glass solutions. Our experts have compiled this comprehensive guide to help you navigate your remodeling journey.

What glass products does Diversity Showers sell? expand_more
We specialize in custom architectural glass solutions including frameless shower enclosures, sliding glass barn doors, custom-cut mirrors, glass railings, and commercial storefronts. All products are manufactured using premium-grade tempered safety glass designed for durability and aesthetic clarity.
Do you offer professional installation services? expand_more
Yes, we provide full white-glove installation services across our service areas. Our team of certified technicians handles everything from precision laser measurements to final structural mounting to ensure your glass installations are perfectly aligned and secure.
What are the lead times for custom glass orders? expand_more
Typically, custom fabrication for tempered glass takes between 10 to 15 business days. Once the glass has cleared our quality control inspection, we coordinate the installation date with you immediately.
What thickness of glass is recommended for shower doors? expand_more
For frameless enclosures, we recommend 10mm (3/8") or 12mm (1/2") tempered glass. These thicknesses provide the structural integrity required for a stable, luxurious feel without the need for heavy metal frames.
How do I maintain and clean my new glass? expand_more
We recommend using a daily squeegee to prevent water spots and lime scale. For cleaning, use a non-abrasive, pH-neutral glass cleaner. Many of our customers opt for a protective coating during fabrication which significantly reduces maintenance requirements.
Do you provide warranties on your glass and hardware? expand_more
Absolutely. All our hardware is covered by a 5-year manufacturer warranty, and we provide a 1-year labor warranty on all installations. We stand by the precision of our craftsmanship.
Can you install glass over existing tile or stone? expand_more
Yes, our specialized mounting hardware is designed to anchor securely into marble, porcelain, granite, and standard ceramic tile. We use diamond-tipped drills to ensure no cracking or damage to your existing surfaces.
What finishes are available for hinges and handles? expand_more
We offer a wide palette including Polished Chrome, Brushed Nickel, Matte Black, Satin Brass, and Oil Rubbed Bronze. All hardware is solid brass or stainless steel for maximum corrosion resistance in humid environments.
